# Hansel and Gretel witch hunters
## Action,Fantasy,Horror
### Description
__ Hansel and Gretel witch hunters__ Hansel & Gretel: Witch Hunters is a movie that follows the siblings Hansel and Gretel, who became bounty hunters of witches after surviving a traumatic childhood encounter. They face a new threat when they discover that a powerful witch is behind the kidnapping of children in a village. The witch plans to sacrifice the abducted children in an ancient magical ceremony during the Blood Moon. As Hansel and Gretel race against time, they unexpectedly uncover a link to their own pasts and the secret their parents took with them when they disappeared
